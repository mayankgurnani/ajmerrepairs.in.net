DIY-Do it yourself

  1. Before calling a technician properly check all the cables and power connections.
  2. Always make sure that your electrical or electronic devices are properly grounded to avoid shock.
  3. Please check all the water connections input and output ports.
  4. Check the mains power supply whether electricity is present there or not.
  5. Always keep in mind to unplug all the home appliances when they are not in use in order to avoid damage resulting due to power surge.
  6. Allow your home appliances to cool down properly after a long operation.
